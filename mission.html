Who are we?

Users can virtually travel to galleries across the world they may not be able to visit otherwise. This can give opportunity to see work and/or inspire art lovers to travel to different galleries and explore different cultures. Inspiration Due to the recent virus pandemic we are unable to travel or gather in public spaces making galleries and museums visits obsolete for the time being. With this application we can continue our pursuit of knowledge and love of art! As a painter, group member Karleigh was looking for a quick way to view many galleries collections. There are articles written on top museums but nowhere to toggle between them in an elegant manner.
